# Privacy Policy — Ozodbek's Music Downloader Bot

_Last updated: 2026_

This bot is provided for educational use. This policy explains what data it
handles.

## What we store

- **Your Telegram user ID** — to recognize returning users.
- **The text or links you send** — used to find the track and (briefly) to
  cache the result so repeat requests are faster.
- **Chat and message IDs** — used only to reply to your request; processed
  transiently and removed after the request is handled.
- **A reference (Telegram file_id) to tracks that were sent** — so the same
  track can be re-sent instantly. No audio files are kept on our server.

We do **not** store your name, phone number, messages to other people, or any
audio files.

## How your request is used

To find a track, your query (a song name or link) is sent to public services
(YouTube, and Spotify's public pages for Spotify links). The audio is fetched
from YouTube. We do not sell or share your data with third parties for
advertising.

## Data retention

Pending requests are deleted as soon as they are processed. Cached track
references are kept to speed up future requests and are automatically pruned
over time.

## Your choices

You can stop using the bot at any time. To request deletion of your stored
user record, contact the developer at @ozodbek_bosimov.

## Changes

This policy may be updated; the latest version will always be available here.
